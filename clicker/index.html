Money deposited in the bank will earn you interest.
This interest is calculated every 10 ticks - but the counter will restart upon deposit or withdrawl.
You will earn interest up to your maximum balance - at which point you can deposit more money, but it will not accrue any interest.

Your maximum balance depends on the number of times you reset your game.
It starts at $10,000,000, and is increased by a power of 10 with each reset - but only if you gain Karma upon resetting.

The bank is happy to have you keep your money with them.
Accordingly, your interest rate will periodically increase based on the total amount of interest you've accrued.
